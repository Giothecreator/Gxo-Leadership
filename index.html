What Are Classical Leadership Styles? Definition

Classical leadership styles are traditional approaches often focused on hierarchy, authority, and structure. || Examples: Autocratic Leadership: One person makes decisions without input from others. Bureaucratic Leadership: Focuses on rules and procedures to maintain order.
